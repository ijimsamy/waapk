Steps to Disable Voice Chat in WhatsApp Groups:

  1. Open WhatsApp: Launch the WhatsApp / GB WhatsApp application on your smartphone.
  2. Access Group Settings: Open the specific group chat where you want to disable voice chat.
  3. Tap Group Info: Click on the group name at the top of the screen to access group information.
  4. Find Voice Chat Settings: Scroll down to locate the "Voice Chats" option.
  5. Toggle Off: If available, turn off the voice chat feature by tapping the toggle switch next to it.
  6. Confirm Changes: Some versions may require you to confirm your choice. Follow any on-screen prompts to complete the process.

Important Notes:

  • - Group Admin Rights: In most cases, only group administrators have the ability to change voice chat settings. If you're not an admin, you may need to request this change from the group admins.
  • - App Version: Ensure your WhatsApp is updated to the latest version, as older versions may not have this feature or the option to disable it.
